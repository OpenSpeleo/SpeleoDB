Data Privacy ~ Transparency ~ Distributed Future

SpeleoDB, developed by the Underwater Speleological and Archaeological Heritage Institute (USAH Institute), is a platform designed to address the unique needs of the cave survey and data collection community. We understand the critical importance of data privacy, ownership, and attribution. Our open-source platform is built on a foundation of transparency, allowing anyone to review the code. SpeleoDB leverages proven solutions like GIT for survey data versioning and champion an open file format standard to promote cross-software compatibility. With a technical steering committee guiding its evolution, the platform emphasizes a decentralized future through a newly developed protocol: SpeleoPUB, fostering seamless collaboration while respecting individual control over data.

Data Privacy

At SpeleoDB, data privacy is our top priority. We understand the community's need for absolute control over their data. Our platform ensures that users maintain ownership and control over their information, with robust privacy measures in place to prevent unauthorized access. By prioritizing data privacy, we provide users with the confidence that their data is protected and handled with the utmost care.

Transparency

SpeleoDB is committed to transparency in all aspects of its operation. As an open-source platform, our code is accessible for anyone to review, ensuring that our practices are open and accountable. This transparency fosters trust within the community, as users can see firsthand how their data is managed and safeguarded. By being transparent, we build a foundation of trust and integrity in our platform.

Powered by GIT

SpeleoDB leverages GIT to version every survey, file or information, ensuring that all changes are tracked and recoverable, making collaboration seamless and reliable.

Forever Recoverable

Each modification is automatically versioned, allowing users to revert to any previous state, ensuring data integrity and historical accuracy.

Secure Backups

All data is backed up across multiple datacenters, providing robust protection against data loss and ensuring accessibility whenever needed.

Open Source Governance

SpeleoDB operates under an open-source model, welcoming contributions from anyone. Join our community and help shape the future of cave surveying and survey collaboration.

Decentralized Future

We aim towards a decentralized future with SpeleoPUB. Run your own server, ensuring data ownership and promoting collaboration across platforms.

Enhanced Survey Workflows

Our platform and tools are designed to streamline and accelerate survey workflows, optimizing collaboration and enhancing efficiency for the entire community.
