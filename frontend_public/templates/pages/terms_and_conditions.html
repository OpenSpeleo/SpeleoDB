Welcome to SpeleoDB, developed by the Underwater Speleological and Archaeological Heritage Institute (USAH Institute). By using our platform, you agree to the following terms and conditions:

Data Privacy

Your data privacy is paramount to us. You retain full ownership and control over your data. SpeleoDB implements robust security measures to prevent unauthorized access, ensuring your data is protected at all times.

Transparency

SpeleoDB is an open-source platform. Our code is publicly available for review, fostering trust and accountability. By using SpeleoDB, you benefit from the transparency of our practices and operations.

Powered by GIT

SpeleoDB utilizes GIT for version control. Every modification to your survey data is tracked and recoverable, ensuring data integrity and facilitating collaboration.

Forever Recoverable

Each change made to your data is versioned, allowing you to revert to any previous state. This ensures that your data remains accurate and recoverable at all times.

Secure Backups

Your data is backed up in multiple datacenters, providing robust protection against data loss. This ensures that your information is always accessible and secure.

Open Source Governance

SpeleoDB operates under an open-source model under the direction of a non-profit organization. We welcome contributions from the community and encourage active participation. Join us in shaping the future of cave surveying.

Decentralized Future

SpeleoDB embraces a decentralized approach through the SpeleoPUB protocol. You have the option to run your own server, ensuring data ownership and fostering a collaborative environment across platforms.

Enhanced Survey Workflows

Our tools are designed to improve and accelerate survey workflows. SpeleoDB optimizes processes, enhancing efficiency and productivity for the cave surveying community.
